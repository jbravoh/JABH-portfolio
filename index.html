About

I am Jay-Ann Bravo-Harriott.

Let's just say I'm multifaceted.

I was a former international basketball player who represented Great Britain, earned an athletic scholarship in the US and played professionally in Spain.

Now I am currently studying a Marketing MSc at Loughborough University alongside working in a media internship for a professional basketball programme. In this role, I utilise my design skills that are self-taught, manage the team's social media accounts and update the programme's website.

Technology has always been an interest of mine and I was fortunate to come across the Black Codher Bootcamp. This is a 6-month programme that provides a community, teaching and mentorship for black females to become full stack developers.

I hope you enjoy my website!
